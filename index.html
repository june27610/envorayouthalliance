ABOUT OUR ACTIVITIES

Explore and get inspired by the activities we worked on, following the smart waste management strategies.

Activity 1
Aeindani Khin

A simple demonstration of practical waste management strategies that park visitors can easily follow during their visit to local parks to help keep the environment clean.

Activity 2
Alinkar Aung Ko

Collecting and picking up waste materials found around the local park to prevent littering and maintain a clean and healthy public space.

Activity 3
San Htet Htar

Gathering discarded waste materials in different areas of the park and disposing of them properly to reduce pollution and protect nature.

Activity 4
Thet Htet Myat Noe Thu

Taking part in collecting litter and waste materials within the local park to promote cleanliness and encourage responsible behavior among visitors.
