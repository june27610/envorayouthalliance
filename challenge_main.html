Smart Challenges

Complete challenges and earn coins, badges, and achievements!

How Gamification Works
Coins

Earn coins by completing challenges and quizzes:

  • Quizzes: 10-50 coins based on score (80%+ = 50 coins + badge)
  • 3-Day Challenge: 5 coins per task (15 coins total)
  • 7-Day Challenge: 10 coins per task (70 coins total)
  • 10-Day Challenge: 10 coins per task (100 coins total)
  • Quantity Challenges: 5 coins per task (15 coins per challenge)
Badges

Unlock badges for achievements:

  • Quiz Master: Score 80%+ on any quiz
  • Challenge Champion: Complete any full challenge
  • Green Guardian: Complete all quantity challenges
  • Consistency King: Complete 7-day or 10-day challenges
