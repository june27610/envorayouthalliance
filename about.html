Why We Exist

ENVORA exists to inspire youth and communities to take an active role in caring for their local environment.

Parks and green spaces are essential for social connection, physical health, and ecological well-being, yet many are underused, neglected, or unsafe. Our alliance fills this gap by creating opportunities for engagement, education, and action, ensuring that communities can enjoy thriving, sustainable natural spaces now and in the future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EYA

Envora Youth Alliance (EYA) is a youth-led initiative that empowers young people and communities to protect, revitalize, and enhance local parks and natural spaces through clean-up activities, education, and sustainable practices.

You can get involved by signing up on our platform, participating in our smart challenges, joining community clean-up events, or taking our educational quizzes to learn more about environmental conservation.

No special skills are required! We welcome everyone who is passionate about making a difference. Our activities are designed to be accessible to people of all ages and backgrounds.

No, participation in ENVORAactivities is completely free. We believe that environmental action should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial means.
